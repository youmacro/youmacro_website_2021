Download Any Video

YouMacro can download any video from your browser.
It's open source and the source and binaries are provided for educational purposes.

Video links can appear as thumbnail images, text or even favicons at the left side of the address bar.

To start downloading:
1) Simply drag the image or text link from your browser onto this app.
2) Or just right click on the image or text link and copy the link address.
